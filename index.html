about the project

Evolution Residence is one of our most representative projects and a symbol of our success.

It represents a distinctive and modern residential complex for individuals, families, and companies, with a commitment to creating an exceptional environment for living, working, and leisure. We have created a unique complex that has been immediately recognized due to high demand and extensive sales dynamics.

The Evolution Residence complex is characterized by top-quality apartments, commercial spaces, exclusive two swimming pools, and a spa center, all built with superior finishing materials, along with a beautiful location surrounded by greenery, providing direct connectivity to all parts of the city.

Aesthetics and Reliability in Every Detail

The corridors and stairwells of the building are clad in elegant porcelain tiles, giving them a refined and modern appearance. Doors of domestic production are equipped with a Grade III anti-burglary system, ensuring a high level of security. The interior doors are faced with MDF and finished with melamine film, combining durability and aesthetic appeal. The flooring is made of three-layer oak parquet, complemented by white MDF baseboards with a height of 6/8 cm, creating a harmonious and stylish interior. Additionally, first-class ceramic tiles from a renowned European manufacturer are used, adding a touch of sophistication and comfort to the overall atmosphere.

How to buy

Credit

Purchasing an apartment in the Evolution Residence housing complex can become a reality with the help of various banks offering favorable loans. Understanding the options available in the market will help you find the best solution for your financial needs.

Buying an apartment on a loan can be a very smart decision for several reasons. First, a home loan allows you to become a property owner without the need to have the entire amount of money upfront. This means you can take advantage of current market opportunities and move into your home faster than if you were to wait until you save the required amount.

Cash

Purchasing an apartment in the Evolution Residence housing complex with cash can be an excellent choice for those who have the means. Paying in cash offers several advantages, including the ability to avoid interest payments and the convenience of a straightforward transaction process.

Choosing to buy with cash means that you can enjoy the benefits of immediate ownership without the need to manage monthly loan repayments. This method not only simplifies the purchase process but also often provides more negotiating power, helping you secure the best possible deal on your new home.
